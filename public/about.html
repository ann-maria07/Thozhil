ABOUT THOZHIL

THOZHIL is a dedicated platform designed to address the unemployment issues faced by middle-aged women and local people in Kerala. Our goal is to connect job seekers with local job opportunities, providing them with easy access to desired jobs. We also assist employers in finding suitable talents by offering a streamlined process for hiring.

OUR MISSION

At THOZHIL, our mission is to empower individuals by providing a centralized platform for job seekers and employers. We aim to connect middle-aged women, local people, and youths in Kerala with local job opportunities and part-time employment. By offering easy access to jobs and promoting social transparency, we strive to improve social welfare and reduce unemployment in society.

OUR SERVICES

  • Access to a wide range of local job opportunities
  • Part-time job listings for flexible employment
  • Simple and efficient job search and application process
  • Job matching based on skills and preferences
  • Transparent and reliable information about job listings
  • Opportunities for employers to find suitable candidates
