Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I sign up for an account on Thozhil.com?

A: To sign up for an account on Thozhil.com, click on the "Sign Up" button on the homepage and follow the instructions to create your account.

Q: How can I search for job listings?

A: To search for job listings, go to the "Jobs" section on the website and use the search bar to enter relevant keywords, location, or job category. You can also use the advanced search filters to refine your search results.

Q: How do I apply for a job listing?

A: To apply for a job listing, navigate to the job details page and click on the "Apply Now" button. Fill out the required application form and attach your resume or any other necessary documents. Click "Submit" to send your application to the employer.

Q: How can I contact the employer directly?

A: To contact the employer directly, go to the job listing that interests you and look for the employer's contact information. This may include their email address or phone number. Reach out to them using the provided contact details for any inquiries or further communication.

Q: Is there a fee for using Thozhil.com?

A:No, there is no fee for using Thozhil.com. We are committed to supporting the well-being and empowerment of individuals seeking employment. Our platform is completely free to use, and we aim to provide equal opportunities for job seekers to connect with employers without any financial barriers.
